# 🚀 Smart Micro-Savings & Referral Engine

A robust, community-driven financial platform designed to facilitate micro-investments and reward-based networking. This system integrates flexible investment contracts, a tiered membership structure, and a 1:1 digital credit economy.

## 📖 Overview
This project provides the core logic for a scalable savings program. Users can participate as free referrers or upgrade to active investors by taking on short-term, high-yield contracts. All system earnings are distributed as digital credits, which hold a 1:1 exact value to PHP.

---

## 👥 Membership Tiers

The system uses a strict two-tier access model to maintain financial sustainability:

### 1. Free Membership (Basic)
* **Access:** 100% Free Registration.
* **Perk:** Generates a unique Referral Code. Earn credits for every successful invite.
* **Limitation:** **No Cash Withdrawal Feature.** Credits earned can only be utilized for internal features (like Mobile Load) or saved until the account is upgraded.

### 2. Membership Plus (Standard Investor)
* **Access:** Requires a minimum account funding of **100 PHP**.
* **Perks:** * Full access to the Referral System.
  * **Unlocked Cash Withdrawals.**
  * Access to the "Smart Contracts" marketplace for credit growth.

---

## 📜 Investment Contracts
Once a user upgrades to **Membership Plus**, they can deploy their funds into available time-locked contracts. 

* **Example Contract: 1-Week Placement**
  * **Duration:** 7 Days
  * **Yield:** 20% ROI credited to the user's dashboard upon maturation.
* **Flexibility:** Users have the autonomy to either **Withdraw** their matured capital and interest, or **Reinvest** it into a new contract for compounding growth.
* *(Note: The system supports adding multiple contract types with varying durations and yields).*

---

## 💰 The Credit Economy
* **Fixed Valuation:** 1 System Credit = 1 PHP.
* **Utility:** * **Cash Out:** Directly withdrawable to bank or e-wallets (for Plus members).
  * **Mobile Load:** Built-in gateway to convert credits into prepaid mobile load across all networks.
  * **Reinvestment:** Use credits to purchase new contracts.
