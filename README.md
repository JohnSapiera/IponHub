# 🚀 IponHub: Smart Micro-Savings & Rewards Engine

**IponHub** is a robust, community-driven digital platform designed to gamify personal savings and reward-based networking. It integrates flexible "Saving Cycles," a tiered membership structure, and a stable 1:1 digital credit economy to help users grow their funds through discipline and community engagement.

---

## 📖 Overview

This project provides the core logic for a scalable, digital saving circle. Users can participate as **Starter Members** by referring others or level up to **VIP Savers** to activate time-based growth challenges. All system rewards are distributed as **Digital Credits**, maintaining a strict 1:1 value ratio with the Philippine Peso (PHP).

---

## 👥 Membership Tiers

The system utilizes a two-tier access model to ensure financial sustainability and reward active participation:

### 1. Starter Member (Basic)
* **Access:** 100% Free Registration.
* **Perk:** Generates a unique **Referral Code**. Earn credits for every successful community invite.
* **Limitation:** Credits earned are stored in the app and can be used for internal features (e.g., Mobile Load) or saved until the account is upgraded to VIP status.

### 2. VIP Saver (Plus)
* **Access:** Requires a one-time account activation of **100 PHP**.
* **Perks:** * **Full Reward Access:** Unlocks the ability to claim/withdraw credits.
    * **Ipon Boosters:** Access to the "Saving Cycles" marketplace for credit growth.
    * **Direct Support:** Priority processing for all transactions.

---

## 📜 Ipon Boosters (Saving Cycles)

Once a user upgrades to **VIP Saver**, they can commit their credits into time-locked challenges designed to reward consistency.

**Example Cycle: 7-Day Quick Growth**
* **Duration:** 7 Days
* **Milestone Reward:** **20% Bonus** credited to the user's dashboard upon maturation.
* **Flexibility:** Users have the autonomy to either **Claim** their matured base and rewards or **Restart** the cycle to compound their growth.
